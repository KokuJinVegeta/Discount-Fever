# Discount-Fever
# Discounting Impact Analysis in eCommerce (Work in Progress)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes how discounting strategies affect sales performance in an eCommerce environment. The goal is to provide stakeholders with a data-driven understanding of when discounts drive incremental revenue versus when they erode value.

The analysis is conducted using a Jupyter Notebook and focuses on transaction-level data, incorporating pricing, discount usage, product segmentation, and time-based trends.

## Project Status: In Progress
This project is currently a **work in progress**.

- Analyses are iterative and may evolve as new insights are uncovered  
- Visualizations and models are subject to refinement  
- Conclusions should be considered **preliminary**  
- Additional validation and robustness checks are planned  

## Objectives
The project aims to answer key business questions:

- Do discounts generate incremental revenue or simply reduce margins?
- Which product segments are most responsive to discounting?
- How does discounting impact sales trends over time?
- Which discount codes or strategies are most effective?

## Analytical Approach
The notebook applies a structured analytical framework:

1. **Time Series Analysis**  
   Evaluate how discounting correlates with sales trends and seasonality  

2. **Segment Analysis**  
   Compare discount sensitivity across price tiers (e.g., premium vs. low-cost products)  

3. **Discount Code Performance**  
   Identify which promotions drive the most revenue  

4. **Revenue Impact Assessment**  
   Analyze whether discounts are incremental or dilutive to total revenue  

## Key Themes (Preliminary)
- Discounts may drive short-term volume increases  
- Price sensitivity varies significantly by segment  
- A small number of discount codes often generate disproportionate impact  
- Overuse of discounts may reduce overall revenue or profitability  

## Intended Audience
This analysis is designed for:

- **Marketing teams** optimizing promotional strategies  
- **Pricing teams** managing price integrity and discount policies  
- **Finance teams** evaluating revenue vs. margin trade-offs  
- **eCommerce leaders** seeking data-driven growth strategies
