# Understanding the Impact of Discounting on eCommerce Sales
Discounting is one of the most commonly used levers in eCommerce to drive sales. However, while discounts can generate short-term increases in volume, their broader effects on revenue, customer behavior, and profitability is often unclear.

This analysis aims to evaluate how discounting influences sales performance across time, product segments, and promotional strategies. By comparing discounted and non-discounted transactions, we seek to determine whether discounts create incremental value or simply shift demand at the expense of margins.
